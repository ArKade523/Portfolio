Graphic Design

I created this design for a T-Shirt contest for my church's youth conference. The theme was 'beach', and our design constraints specified that we had to include the words 'A Great Work' somewhere in the design.
One of the most common uses of my Graphic Design abilities is creating logos for Discord Servers and group chats. This is my favorite of the ones I have made. I like the color combination, and the animation turned out better than I could have hoped. This logo was made for a math server that I am an admin of, so that connection makes it feel even better each time I look at it.
